# Callout Palette

Overview of custom callouts used across the Cognitive Architecture publish site. Update this table when adding new callouts or changing their purpose.

| Callout | Icon | Purpose | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| info | `lucide-compass` | Highlight contextual information or guidance for readers navigating the garden. | Default Obsidian styling with custom icon. |
| warning | `lucide-shield-alert` | Surface disclaimers, caveats, or critical considerations. | Used on `Home.md` for site-wide disclaimer. |
| tldr | `lucide-book-open` | Provide summaries upfront for longer sections. | Present on `The Greenhouse.md` and `The Grove.md`. |
| gate | `lucide-fence` | Mark entry points or gatekeeping notes that direct readers to curated areas. | Available for future use where gated context is helpful. |
